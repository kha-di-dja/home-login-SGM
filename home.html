Welcome to SGM

Smart Students Grades Management System

This website is designed to help manage student grades in a high school. It provides an easy way to organize student information, subjects, and their results, all in one place. The interface is straightforward, making it accessible for both administrators and teacher.

Our Services

📖 Students Management

Register new students, subjects, or grades and save them to the database.

✏️ Subjects Management

Edit existing student information, subjects, or grades when needed.

📊 Results Management

Delete unwanted or incorrect student information, subjects, or grades.

🏆 Ranking Page

Display all grades and the average of a selected student.
